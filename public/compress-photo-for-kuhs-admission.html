Why KUHS photo requirements are strict

KUHS handles applications for thousands of medical and health science students every year. Standardized photo dimensions and file sizes help the university maintain uniform records across colleges and courses.

Practical tips for KUHS applicants

  • Use a recent photo with clear facial features.
  • JPEG format usually works best.
  • Avoid scanned photos with shadows.
  • Keep a backup copy.
