Why KUHS photo requirements are strict

KUHS handles applications for thousands of medical and health science students every year. Standardized photo dimensions and file sizes help the university maintain uniform records across colleges and courses. Even a small mismatch can cause upload failures or delays during document verification.

Practical tips for KUHS applicants

  • Use a recent photo with clear facial features and even lighting.
  • JPEG format usually works best for health university portals.
  • Avoid scanned photos with shadows or visible borders.
  • Keep a backup copy once your photo is accepted.
