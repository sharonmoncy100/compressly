Compress photo for Kerala PSC (KPSC) - step by step

Prepare profile photo and signature to meet KPSC Thulasi upload limits and pixel rules. Private and fast browser-only tool.

Kerala PSC applications often require strict limits on photo file size and sometimes pixel dimensions. This short guide shows how to compress your photo and signature so they are accepted by KPSC and other government portals.

- Quick guide

  1. Open Compressly home (Open Compressor) and choose your image.
  2. If the portal requires an exact pixel size, crop/resize first (for example 150 x 200 px).
  3. Enter a target in KB (for example 100 KB) and press Compress.
  4. Download and upload to KPSC Thulasi portal. If rejected, reduce target to 90 KB and try again.
Note: KPSC may ask for both photo and signature files. Signature images are often PNG with transparency - see our signature guide for best results.

- Tips for KPSC image uploads

  • Use JPEG for photos unless portal explicitly requires PNG.
  • For transparent signatures, compress PNG carefully or convert to JPG if transparency not required.
  • Try WebP if the portal supports it for smaller file sizes.

- FAQ

Q: What is the ideal target for KPSC photos?
A: Start with 100 KB. If the portal restricts further, lower to 80-90 KB.

Q: Can I keep the original pixel dimensions?
A: Yes, but downscaling slightly (to 1200 px on longest side) often helps reduce file size with little quality loss.
