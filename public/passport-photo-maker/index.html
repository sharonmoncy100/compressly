What Is a Passport Size Photo?

A passport size photo is a standardised portrait photograph used for official identification documents. In India, the standard size is 35×45 mm (approximately 3.5 cm × 4.5 cm) printed at 300 DPI, which corresponds to 413×531 pixels at that resolution. The photo should show a front-facing view with a plain background, with the face clearly visible from chin to crown.

This format is required for Indian passports, Aadhaar card applications, PAN card applications, driving license applications, government job applications, competitive exam forms, college admissions, and visa applications.
