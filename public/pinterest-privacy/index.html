Pinterest Image Downloader Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

This Privacy Policy applies specifically to the Pinterest Image Downloader provided by Compressly. It explains how information is handled when you use this tool.

For information about the privacy practices of Compressly's other image tools, please see our general Privacy Policy.

How the Pinterest Image Downloader works

When you submit a supported Pinterest URL, the URL is sent to our server-side service so that the tool can process the request and identify publicly accessible image information associated with the submitted URL.

When you request an image download, our service may temporarily retrieve and transfer the requested image to your browser to enable the download.

Information you provide

The primary information you provide to the tool is the Pinterest URL that you choose to submit. You do not need to create a Compressly account or provide your name, email address, or other personal information to use the downloader.

How submitted URLs are used

Submitted URLs are used to process your request and retrieve the publicly accessible information necessary for the tool to function.

Compressly does not intentionally create a permanent personal library or collection of the Pinterest URLs submitted by users through the downloader.

Image processing and downloads

Compressly does not provide permanent user storage for images downloaded through the Pinterest Image Downloader. Images requested through the tool may pass through server infrastructure as part of processing and delivering the requested download.

Compressly does not claim ownership of images or other content obtained from third-party platforms. Rights in third-party content remain with their respective owners.

Technical logs

Like most online services, our hosting and infrastructure providers may automatically process limited technical information required to operate, secure, troubleshoot, and maintain the service. This may include information such as request timestamps, IP addresses, browser or device information, requested URLs, and technical error information.

The availability and retention of such technical information may depend on the infrastructure and service providers used by Compressly.

Analytics

We may use analytics and performance-monitoring services to understand how the tool is used and to improve its reliability and performance. This may include information such as page views, download events, device type, browser information, and general geographic region.

Analytics data is used to understand overall usage patterns and improve Compressly's services.

The Pinterest Image Downloader interacts with publicly accessible resources provided through third-party platforms. Those third-party services have their own terms and privacy practices, which are outside Compressly's control.
