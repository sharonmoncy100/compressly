Why photo compression matters

University admission portals use strict validation to reduce server load and ensure uniformity across applications. Even a small difference in file size or dimensions can cause upload failures. Compressly helps you meet these limits while keeping the photo clear and professional.

Helpful tips for Kerala University applicants

  • Use JPEG format for best compatibility with admission portals.
  • Avoid scanned photos with heavy shadows or borders.
  • If the file size is strict, reduce quality slightly instead of resizing too much.
  • Save a backup copy before uploading.

Frequently asked questions

What photo size does Kerala University require?

Requirements can vary by course and admission cycle. Most Kerala University portals specify a maximum file size and sometimes fixed pixel dimensions. Always confirm the official notification before uploading.

Can I use Compressly on my phone?

Yes. Compressly works fully in your mobile browser and supports photos from your gallery or camera.

Will compressing reduce photo clarity?

Light compression does not noticeably affect clarity. Cropping properly and avoiding extreme compression helps maintain a clear facial image.
