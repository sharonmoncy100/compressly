Frequently asked

What exact pixel size and file size does Kerala PSC need?

Common PSC-style requirements are 200×150px for photo and file sizes often required ≤30KB - confirm the specific notice for your notification. Compressly helps you hit those numbers.

Can I use Compressly on mobile?

Yes - Compressly runs entirely in your browser on mobile; pick a photo from the gallery. The tool is tuned for low-end phones too.

Will compression reduce face clarity?

A small amount of visible quality loss is possible when forcing a very low KB target. To keep clarity: crop tightly, prefer JPEG, and try several quality settings (85 → 70 → 60) until the face remains clear.
