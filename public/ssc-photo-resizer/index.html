SSC Photo & Signature Requirements

SSC CGL and SSC CHSL online applications require you to upload a recent passport-size photograph and a handwritten signature. Both must meet exact pixel dimensions and file size limits — incorrect images can get your form rejected.

This tool automatically resizes and compresses your image to SSC-ready specifications. Upload, crop, and download — all in seconds, without any server upload.

All processing happens locally in your browser. Your image is never uploaded or stored on any server.
