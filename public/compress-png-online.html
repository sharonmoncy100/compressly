Compress PNG Online - Kerala PSC, University and Exam Uploads

PNG images are clear and high quality but they usually have a large file size. Many Indian portals such as Kerala PSC, university admission sites, exam registrations and scholarship websites reject large PNG files. Compressly helps you compress PNG images quickly or convert PNG to JPG or WebP for an even smaller result.

How to compress PNG files

  1. Open Compressly.
  2. Choose your PNG image.
  3. Select PNG for lossless compression, or select JPG or WebP for a smaller output file.
  4. Click Compress to download your optimized image.
