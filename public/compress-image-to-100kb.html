Many online applications in India, especially Kerala PSC, Government exams, University portals, job recruitment sites and various entrance exams require the photo to be less than 100 KB. Compressly helps you compress your image to exactly 100 KB without losing much quality. Everything works inside your browser, so your photo never gets uploaded to any server.

How to compress an image to 100 KB

  1. Open Compressly (Home).
  2. Choose your image (JPG, PNG or WebP).
  3. In the Target (KB) box, type 100.
  4. Press the Compress button.
  5. Download the 100 KB image instantly.
