What Is a Pinterest Image Downloader?

A Pinterest image downloader is an online tool that lets you save photos and images from Pinterest pins directly to your device. Pinterest does not offer a built-in download button for most images, which means users often rely on screenshots - a method that gives you a low-resolution, cropped result. Our free tool bypasses this limitation by fetching the original high-quality image file directly from Pinterest's servers.

Whether you are a designer looking for mood board inspiration, a student collecting reference images, a marketer saving visual assets, or simply someone who loves a particular photo, this tool lets you save any public Pinterest pin in full HD quality with one click.

How to Copy a Pinterest Pin URL

On a desktop browser, simply navigate to the Pinterest pin you want, and copy the URL from your browser's address bar. It will look like: https://www.pinterest.com/pin/123456789/

On the Pinterest mobile app (iPhone or Android), open the pin, tap the three-dot menu or the Share button, and choose "Copy Link." Both full Pinterest URLs and shortened pin.it links are supported by our tool.

Is It Legal to Download Pinterest Images?

You can technically download any publicly visible image using our tool. However, it is your responsibility to respect copyright. Many images on Pinterest are owned by photographers, designers, and artists. Always credit the original creator if you share the image elsewhere, and never use downloaded content for commercial purposes without permission from the rights holder.

Our tool is designed for personal use cases such as saving inspiration, creating offline mood boards, or backing up your own pins. If you are uncertain about the rights to an image, reach out to the original creator before using it.

Why Not Just Take a Screenshot?

Screenshots capture only what is visible on your screen at your current display resolution - typically a compressed, low-quality preview. Pinterest shows thumbnail-sized images in the feed and even on individual pin pages. Using our downloader, you get access to the full original resolution image that the uploader submitted to Pinterest, which can be significantly larger and sharper than what appears on screen.
