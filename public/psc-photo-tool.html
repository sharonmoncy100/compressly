PSC Photo Requirements

Kerala PSC applications require photos to meet strict pixel and file-size rules. Images outside the allowed range may be rejected by the Thulasi portal.

This tool prepares photos using Kerala PSC official standards: 150×200 pixels for passport photos, 150×100 pixels for signatures, and 30 KB file size.

All processing happens locally in your browser. Your image is never uploaded or stored on any server.
